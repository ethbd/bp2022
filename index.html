We exist to make that possible. At Ethicon, no boundary is impossible to push, no future is unreachable, and no problem is too hard to solve. We believe that every incision and decision can be driven by individuality.
Each action we take is an answer to surgery’s biggest questions: What’s the right solution for this person? How can we help clinicians adapt to meet unique needs? How can we reshape standards of care?
It’s a human approach to solving human problems—helping care teams orchestrate the most effective way to treat each patient, even if that way doesn’t yet exist.
We’re continuing our century-long pursuit of transforming surgery by empowering better outcomes for everyone, everywhere.
